Quick Q+A

I noticed you don’t offer [this flavour], is it possible to be made?

It depends. I ask that if the flavour you're interested in is not listed here, please ask to see if I'm able to make it.

Do you offer delivery services?

If you are located within a worthy distance, I can deliver the goods at a small fee. Otherwise, I offer, and much prefer, pickup.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! I offer catering services on all goods sold here. Please inquire about that and give a good timeframe for needing the order.

Is a deposit required for ordering?

Yes, I require at the minimum 3/4 of the price of the goods before starting to bake, whatever is left is to be paid when the goods are received.
